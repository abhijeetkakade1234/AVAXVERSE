# Missions

High-stakes protocol operations secured by state-of-the-art smart contract escrows. Avalanche's premier marketplace for trustless execution.

## Core Pillars
- **Sovereign Escrows**: Smart contracts hold funds in deterministic state machines.
- **Milestone Enforcement**: Payments are released only upon verifiable proof of work.
- **DAO Arbitration**: Disputes are escalated to a decentralized jury for final resolution.
- **Reputation Layer**: Success increases your on-chain standing and matching weight.

## Operational Flow
1. **Briefing**: Client posts a mission with requirements and budget.
2. **Assignment**: Candidates apply; Client selects an Operator.
3. **Commitment**: Operator accepts and stakes.
4. **Execution**: Work is delivered and artifacts are submitted.
5. **Resolution**: Client approves or disputes.
